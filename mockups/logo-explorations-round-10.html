round 10 — revisiting 3A

5 takes on the hand-painted brush direction

Stepped back from the court-block direction to revisit 3A — Permanent Marker wordmark with a tracked Anton tagline. The original was a quick exploration; these five iterations tighten the type, vary the supporting elements, and try the mark at navbar scale.

The constants: Permanent Marker for the wordmark, Anton for the tagline, court-red ampersand (yellow on dark stages). The variables: supporting marks (swing, rule, none), tagline placement, and density.
