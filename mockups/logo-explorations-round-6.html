round 6 — pickleball court blocks

B5, made literal — Bert & Erne, opposite sides of the net

Six takes on the three-block lockup with the blocks drawn as a pickleball court: BERT on one side, & as the net, ERNE on the other. Kitchen line (the dink-zone boundary) sits close to the net on each side; centerline divides the two service boxes on each half.

Different surface colors per variant — outdoor green, pro blue, clay, two-team split, and a minimal cream schematic at the end.
