round 9 — stamp + bookends

10 takes on V1 × V4

You loved V1 (corner stamp) and V4 (bookend brackets in tag row). Every variant below carries both ideas — a stamp somewhere up top, and brackets flanking the tagline. The variable is which stamp, what colour, and how the bookends respond.

Court cleanup: centerlines removed — just kitchen yellow + net + the black bones. No bracket sits on a letter. The bookends stay inside the tag row; the stamps hang off corners or the top edge.
