round 2 — sport, not boutique

bert & erne — 10 more directions

Round 1 read as high-end furniture / dress shop. This round swings the other way: varsity, stadium, stencil, league-pennant, bumper sticker — the visual tradition of actual sport graphics. Brighter colors (court yellow, hot court blue, league red) and chunkier type. The bar: looks at home on a tournament t-shirt back, screen-printed in a single color, three feet wide on a draw board.

Still no literal paddles / balls / courts (per the original brief). The name does the joke; the typography carries the energy.
