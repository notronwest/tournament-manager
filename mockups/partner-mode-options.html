Partner-mode picker — seven alternatives

The "Pick a partner / I need a partner" toggle in PublicTournamentPage.tsx sits inside an expanded register form on every doubles event. Either choice swaps the subform below — picker shows a search field, "need a partner" shows a blue info panel.

PR #58 turned the two buttons into a connected segmented control. They still read as buttons. Seven directions below to consider — each option shows both states (picker-active above, need-active below) inside a mock register form.

The job to be done: tell the system "I'm registering with X" or "find me a partner." Once you've answered that, the form below should obviously change. Today the answer space looks like two action buttons; we want it to look like a choice that's been made.
01 · Radio cards

Stacked radio rows with descriptions

Classic "choose one" semantics — the dots make "this is a selection, not an action" unambiguous.
